Welcome to WeddingSnap

Let's get your system configured in 3 easy steps.

📷 Step 1: Camera Configuration

To ensure lightning-fast transfers without sacrificing your high-quality edits later, configure your camera's dual card slots:

  • Set Slot 1 to capture RAW format (for your post-processing).
  • Set Slot 2 to capture Small/Medium JPEG (for WeddingSnap).
  • Connect your camera to this laptop via a high-speed USB-C tether cable.

💾 Step 2: Master Storage

Where should WeddingSnap save all the photos?

Select an external SSD or a dedicated folder on this laptop. We will automatically create the required database and gallery folders inside it.

📡 Step 3: Smart Router

Connect your official networking hardware.

To host the local gallery, this laptop must be connected to your WeddingSnap Smart Router.

  • Plug the router into power.
  • Connect this laptop to the router's Wi-Fi network (or use a LAN cable for maximum stability).
